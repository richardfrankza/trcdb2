An archive of data about violations of Human Rights 1994 - 1998

 

Statements

The Commission gathered tens of thousands of statements about gross violations of human rights during its lifespan.

Amnesty

Perpetrators of violations of human rights were given an opportunity to apply for amnesty.

Acts

Every act of a gross violation of Human Rights that was reported to the TRC was recorded by data capturers.

Maps

Maps are available to locate the incidences of human rights violations across the country.

 

Victims

The database lists every victim that was reported to the TRC.

Perpetrators

Perpetrators of violations of human rights were gathered from Amnesty Applications and witness Statements.

Witnesses

The names of all witnesses were recorded for the purposes of corroboration.

Help

Includes a history of the TRC, a glossary of terms and instructions about the use of the site.
